How Involve PH Works

Involve PH bridges the gap between citizens and the local government. By making reporting simple and transparent, we ensure that every concern is heard and acted upon.

Submit a Report

Capture the issue with a photo or video and share its location through the platform.

LGU Receives It

Your report is instantly forwarded to the appropriate local government office.

Assessment

Authorities verify the issue and determine the appropriate response.

Resolution

The issue is resolved and citizens are updated with progress and completion.
