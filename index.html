How Involve PH Works


Citizen reports an issue

Submit a report with details and photos using the platform.

LGU receives the report

The local government unit receives and logs the report instantly.

Issue is reviewed

The report is verified and assessed by the assigned office.

Action is taken

The issue is resolved and progress is updated for citizens..
